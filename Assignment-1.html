Variable Transformation

Assist to turnover ration (AST_TOV) variable made by dividing assists per game by turnovers per game, measures players efficiency in creating assists while limiting turnovers.

nba$AST_TOV <- nba$AST / nba$TOV

Histogram

This histogram shows the distribution of points per game for all NBA players. Most of the players in the league scored between 0 and 10 points, whereas a very small percentage scored above 25 points per game. This is as expected.

hist(nba$PTS,
     main = "Distribution of Points Per Game",
     xlab = "Points Per Game")

Scatterplot

The scatterplot shows the relationship between assists per game and points per game. The plot suggests a positive relationship with players who average a higher number of assists also average more points per game. However, most players average a low number of assists and points per game still.

plot (nba$AST,
      nba$PTS,
      main = "Assists Per Game vs Points Per Game",
      xlab = "Assists Per Game",
      ylab = "Points Per Game")
